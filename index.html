Biography


Dr. Liyan Ma is an Associate Professor in School of Computer Engineering and Science, Shanghai University. She received the Ph.D. degree from Beijing Jiaotong University in 2013, under the supervision of Prof. Jian Yu. She obtained Master degree from China University of Geosciences in 2008, under the supervision of Pro. Hongwei Li.
Dr. Liyan Ma is now mainly working on the computer vision for robot system and medical imaging, such as object recognition and image matching algorithms for patrol robot, few-shot learning, image restoration and panoptic segmentation for unmanned surface vessel, disease degrading for automated detection of Diabetic Retinopathy and Pathology Image Analysis. Recently, working with our partner from CUHK, our team won the fourth place in the 2nd Diabetic Retinopathy – Grading and Image Quality Estimation Challenge organized by ISBI 2020.
We are looking for self-motivated graduate candidates and undergraduate students, who have good mathematical and coding skills, to join us. The graduate candidates will have the opportunity to visit CUHK as Joint training MSc programs. Our team expects to solve some real problems and make other people be benefited from our works.
