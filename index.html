Academic

2020.01-2020.03 the 2nd Diabetic Retinopathy

    International Symposium on Biomedical Imaging(ISBI 2020) held the 2nd diabetic retinopathy detection competition. Before the competition, I learned the relevant knowledge about the classification of conventional fundus images and read the relevant master's thesis to understand the common methods of classification on this type of data set. Finally,a comparative experiment is made between the ensemble model of Alexnet and GoogleNet and the EfficientNet, and the ensemble method is used as the competition model on the efficient netb3-5. The fifth leaderboard and Online Challenge are ranked fourth. After the end of the competition, I use the last two years' fine-grained classification algorithm, such as the improved model of the bilinear model and the multi granularity model, to further experiment and optimize the dataset. In addition, I also use the method of addressing class imbalance, such as sampling and cost sensitive. At present, the current result is significantly higher than that of the competition in the validation set.
